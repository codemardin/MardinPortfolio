About Me

S. M. Mardin Rahman

I am S. M. Mardin Rahman, a dedicated Science student at Notre Dame College, Dhaka, driven by a deep curiosity and passion for understanding the world. My academic foundation has strengthened my critical thinking and problem-solving abilities, allowing me to excel in diverse scientific and creative fields.
